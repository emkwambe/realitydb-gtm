RealityDB (Mpingo Systems LLC). Companion to `RDB-SALES-002-US-Prospect-Pipeline.md`
(direct enterprise outreach) — this brief targets **consulting/agency firms**
as referral partners rather than end buyers.

firms yielded a verified, named, *Charlotte-specific* Data & Analytics or GRC
practice lead with a confirmed LinkedIn URL in every case. Several firms

office at all**. Where a name/LinkedIn is marked "unconfirmed," verify
